Waterfall

2018

2021 Feeel Design Award - longlisted

Concept

Modern asian style

Inspired by the classic eastern style shelf, which was designed for merchandise objects. Transform into a modern style for fitting in our living place.

Process

Sheet metal bending

To create the form of water flowing, the platforms are made of metal sheets with huge radius bending. The wireframe system provides structure and borders. Powder coating makes the shelf more durable and has an anti-slip surface.
