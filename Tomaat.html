Tomaat

2021

Design for Treekind, 2021

Concept

Organizer

This project works with Biophilica and Bluecity. The material is Treekind®, a new plant-based, recyclable, compostable, low-resource leather alternative that is completely free of plastic polyurethane.

The design of this series is like origami, formed with one piece of leather. I would like to design beautiful home accessories that can merge into today's modern style. To show the beauty of the leather, I think polyhedron is a good way to show various angles and tension on the sur- faces.

Process

Hand craft

It contains decent craftsmanship: hand stitching; crease stamping; weaving. The design is trying to learn from natural growing. For instance, in the design, the bottom is a rectangle but top is octagon so the body is gradually enlarging.
