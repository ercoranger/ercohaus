Presented in Not Just Library 2018
Exhibited in Creative Expo Taiwan 2018

Concept

Industrial Geometry

This is a project works with a 25-year OEM factory in Taiwan.
This local factory fabricates the machine’s shell and structure for living. Concept is based on what they have already been doing and use the material they are familiar with. Inspired by large material dispensers. Hopper has an ergonomic slanted design which makes reaching for a pen effortless.

Process

Sheet metal manufacturing

The factory is skilful at laser cutting, metal sheet folding, and welding. Using laser cutting to create precise slots and minimal bending to form a container. Color ranges from gavanized steel sheet to powder coating. you can check more objects from here.
