Exhibited in Object Rotterdam 2022

Introduction

Based on Research of Neo Stone Age, I develop a new technique repairing broken glassware or ceramic with “Neostone”. From this new “kintsugi” craft, I give those unwanted homewares a mineral coating.

I covered broken glass objects with a material that I developed using discarded lime pellet, powder of limestone from quarries and sand from construction sites, called geo-polymer, also known as "Neostone", this material doesn’t require firing and adds a new touch and feeling to the object.

What to design for a world of plenty? Natural resources are getting exhausted. I believe in alternative ways designing, using what is already there. This results in a local and circular production.
