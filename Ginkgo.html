Ginkgo

2024

2024 Presented in Sotckholm Furniture fair

Concept

Shapes of leaves

Gingko is a wooden chair inspired by the soothing form of leaves. Harmoniously blending botanical inspiration with functionality, its quarter-circle seat design not only provides a comfortable seating experience but also offers versatility in placement, effortlessly fitting into any corner of a room. Its genuine design brings a touch of nature indoors, invoking tranquility and balance through its neat geometric form.

Process

Hand craft

Crafted from sustainably sourced wood, each Gingko chair undergoes meticulous craftsmanship to showcase the natural beauty of the material. Skilled artisans sculpt the wood, ensuring that every chair reflects the intricate patterns and textures found in gingko leaves. The result is a chair that not only embodies elegance but also respects the environment through responsible sourcing and manufacturing practices.

Special Edition

Aluminum

In addition to its classic wooden iteration, Gingko presents a special edition featuring aluminum accents. This contemporary twist enhances Gingko's aesthetic appeal, infusing it with a modern edge while retaining its minimalist charm. Whether placed in a sleek urban setting or a cozy countryside retreat, the Gingko special edition offers a stylish seating solution that complements various interior styles.
